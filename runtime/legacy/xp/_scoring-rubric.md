# Scoring Rubric

## Per-Image Scoring (1-10)

### Product Accuracy (weight: 30%)
- 10: Product identical to input photo (shape, color, details)
- 8: 90%+ match, minor differences acceptable
- 6: Recognizable but noticeable deviations
- 4: Clearly distorted, hard to recognize
- 2: Completely wrong product

### Visual Quality (weight: 25%)
- 10: Professional studio quality, sharp, well-lit
- 8: High quality, minor imperfections
- 6: Acceptable, some artifacts/blur
- 4: Low quality, visible AI artifacts
- 2: Unusable

### Composition & Layout (weight: 20%)
- 10: Perfect balance, clear hierarchy, text readable
- 8: Good layout, minor spacing issues
- 6: Acceptable but cramped or unbalanced
- 4: Messy, overlapping elements
- 2: No clear structure

### Conversion Relevance (weight: 15%)
- 10: Directly addresses buyer concern for this slot
- 8: Relevant, strong selling point
- 6: Generic but acceptable
- 4: Missed the point of the slot
- 2: Wrong content for slot function

### Brand Consistency (weight: 10%)
- 10: Perfect match with brief (colors, mood, style)
- 8: Mostly consistent
- 6: Partially matches
- 4: Off-brand
- 2: Completely wrong style

---

## Calculation
Final Score = (ProductAccuracy * 0.30) + (VisualQuality * 0.25) + (Composition * 0.20) + (ConversionRelevance * 0.15) + (BrandConsistency * 0.10)

## Thresholds
- **>=7.0** — PASS
- **<7.0** — NEEDS IMPROVEMENT
- **<5.0** — REDO
