## KIE Quality Loss Root Cause

Observed likely quality drop in KIE workflow comes from two internal compression/resizing stages:

1. Product refs uploaded to KIE are downscaled in `runtime/legacy/utils/kie-client.js` via `uploadFromLocal()` to max `768px`, `quality: 85`, usually converted to JPEG.
2. Final A+ outputs are rewritten by runtime in `runtime/legacy/agents/aplus-pipeline.js` and `runtime/legacy/utils/image-utils.js` using Sharp resize/crop to exactly `1464x600` and JPEG `quality: 95`.

Implication: image downloaded from KIE may already be okay, but local runtime post-processing and low-res ref upload can reduce sharpness / fidelity.
